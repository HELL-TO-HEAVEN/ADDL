The input file of all scripts here is the .csv test report file generated by 
function evaluate() in ../4.ModelTrainTest/subject_level_classification_2classes.py.

s1_continuousN1_as_AD: Continuous N AD as AD of subject
s2a_windowN: In window of size N, all AD as AD of window; Window_AD > Window_NL as AD of subject
s2b_windowN: In window of size N, PNG_AD > PNG_NL as AD of window; Window_AD > Window_NL as AD of subject
s3_windowN: Look at only N png from bottom of brain, PNG_AD > PNG_NL as AD of subject
